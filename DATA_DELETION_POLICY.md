# Data Deletion Request for Resume AI Pro

## About Resume AI Pro

Resume AI Pro is an AI-powered resume builder application available on the Google Play Store. All user data is stored **locally on your device** — we do not maintain any backend servers or cloud database. When you use Google Sign-In, we only store your profile information (name, email, photo) in your device's local database.

## How to Request Data Deletion

### Step 1: Open Resume AI Pro
Open the **Resume AI Pro** app on your Android device.

### Step 2: Access Settings
1. Tap the **Settings** icon (gear icon, usually in the menu)
2. Scroll to find **Account & Privacy** or **Data Management**

### Step 3: Delete Your Account & Data
1. Tap on **Delete My Account & Data**
2. You will see a warning explaining that this action will:
   - Permanently delete all resumes you created
   - Permanently delete all job matches and application tracker data
   - Remove your cached Google profile information
   - Reset the app to its initial state
3. Tap **Confirm Deletion** to proceed
4. Your data will be immediately wiped from your device

### Step 4: Confirmation
Once deletion is complete, the app will reset and show the initial setup screen on next launch.

## What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, **all local data on your device is permanently removed**, including:

- ✅ All resumes and cover letters you created
- ✅ All job matches and application tracker entries
- ✅ Your cached Google profile (name, email, photo)
- ✅ Your saved work experience, education, and skills
- ✅ Your app preferences and settings
- ✅ All locally stored application history

## What Data Is NOT Stored by Resume AI Pro

Resume AI Pro **does not store or maintain**:

- ❌ **No backend server account** - We don't have servers storing your data
- ❌ **No cloud database** - All data lives only on your device
- ❌ **No account on Resume AI Pro servers** - Your Google Sign-In creates no remote account

## Third-Party Data Processing

Resume AI Pro integrates with third-party services for specific features. These services receive data only when you use their features:

| Service | What Data Is Sent | When | Stored By Them? |
|---------|------------------|------|-----------------|
| **Google Gemini API** | Resume text, job descriptions | When you analyze a job match | No - API call only, not stored by us |
| **Jooble** | Job search keywords | When you search for jobs | No - search results only |
| **Adzuna** | Job search keywords | When you search for jobs | No - search results only |
| **Google Sign-In** | Your Google profile (with your permission) | When you sign in | Only stored locally on your device |

**Note**: Resume AI Pro does not store API responses from these services on any server. Data sent to these third parties is governed by their own privacy policies.

## Placeholder Values (Not Real)

The following fields in your local database are currently **placeholder values only** and are not connected to any real service:

- `stripeCustomerId` - Placeholder (no real Stripe account)
- `stripeSubscriptionId` - Placeholder (no real Stripe integration)

These do not represent any actual payment or subscription data.

## Data Deletion Is Immediate and Complete

- **No waiting period** - Your data is deleted immediately upon confirmation
- **Permanent** - Once deleted, data cannot be recovered (it's not stored anywhere else)
- **Local only** - Since all data is on your device, deletion is final and complete
- **No server-side cleanup needed** - We have no servers to delete from

You have the right to:
- ✅ Delete all your data at any time using the in-app deletion tool
- ✅ Know exactly what data we store (only your device's local database)
- ✅ Understand third-party data processing (see table above)
- ✅ Request information about how your data is used

Resume AI Pro:
- ❌ Does **not** sell your data
- ❌ Does **not** share your resumes with recruiters or employers without your permission
- ❌ Does **not** use your data for marketing or profiling
- ❌ Does **not** track you across other apps

## Having Trouble Deleting Your Data?

If you cannot find the deletion option or need help:

1. **Update the app** - Ensure you have the latest version of Resume AI Pro
2. **Force close the app** - Go to Settings > Apps > Resume AI Pro > Force Stop
3. **Reopen and try again** - Launch Resume AI Pro and navigate to Settings > Delete Account & Data
4. **Clear app data manually** - Go to Settings > Apps > Resume AI Pro > Storage > Clear Data (this will delete the local database)
5. **Uninstall and reinstall** - This will remove all app data from your device
