Privacy Policy

Resume AI Pro

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Resume AI Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, including our ATS scoring, job search, resume optimization, and CV management features.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password when you create an account, or basic profile information when you sign in via Google.
  • Resume and Career Data: Resumes you upload or create, career history, skills, education, employment details, and any other content you add to your profile or CV Vault.
  • Job Search Information: Your job preferences, location preferences, salary expectations, search filters, and jobs you view or apply to.
  • Application Tracking Data: Information about jobs you've applied to, including job titles, companies, dates, and application status.
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests, or inquiries you send to us.
  • Purchase Information: Information about premium subscriptions and individual purchases processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store credit card details; Google Play handles all payment processing securely.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device settings.
  • Usage Data: Features used, resume scores viewed, jobs searched or applied to, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns.
  • Log Data: Server logs including IP addresses, access times, error logs, and pages/features accessed.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address and, if enabled, precise location for job search filtering purposes.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies, analytics tools, and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze app performance.
  • Resume Analysis Data: Information about how the ATS scoring system analyzes your resume (keywords detected, formatting issues, skill matches).
  • Advertising Data: If Google Ads are enabled, Google may collect advertising identifiers, device data, and usage patterns to serve personalized ads.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Job Listing Data: Job postings sourced from Jooble and Adzuna APIs, including job titles, descriptions, company information, location, and salary data.
  • Google Services: If you sign in via Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email). Google Play Billing provides transaction and subscription status information. Google Ads may share advertising performance data.
  • Analytics Providers: Data from analytics services to understand app usage and performance.
  • Advertising Networks: Information from Google Ads and other ad networks for measurement and personalization purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving Resume AI Pro features (ATS scoring, resume analysis, job search, application tracking)
  • Creating and managing your account and profile
  • Authenticating you via Google Sign-In
  • Processing subscriptions and purchases through Google Play Billing
  • Analyzing your resume against job postings and generating ATS scores with transparent, weighted feedback
  • Identifying hidden keywords and industry-standard terms relevant to your career
  • Generating targeted interview questions to surface relevant experience
  • Automatically optimizing your resume for specific job listings
  • Powering job search functionality across Jooble and Adzuna listings
  • Managing your CV Vault and application tracking history
  • Serving and personalizing ads through Google Ads
  • Sending technical notices, updates, and administrative messages
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve functionality and user experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our agreements
  • Marketing and promotional purposes (with your consent)

4. Resume and Career Data Protection

Your resume and career data is particularly sensitive. We treat this information with the highest level of security and care:

  • Your resumes are stored securely in your personal CV Vault and are not shared with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • Resume analysis (ATS scoring, keyword detection, optimization suggestions) is performed to improve our recommendations to you only.
  • When you use "Tailor Resume to This Job," the optimized resume is created for your use; the original job posting data and tailored version are not shared with employers unless you explicitly apply through our "Quick Apply" feature.
  • Application tracking data is maintained in your personal dashboard for your reference only.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your resume data to recruiters, employers, or other third parties.
  • Resume data is excluded from Google Ads targeting and personalization.

5. Google Play Billing and Purchases

Resume AI Pro offers premium monthly subscriptions and individual feature purchases through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have direct access to your credit card or payment method information.
  • Transaction Data: We receive information about your subscription status, purchase history, and renewal dates to manage your account and provide access to premium features.
  • Billing Information: Google Play provides billing information necessary to process refunds, manage subscriptions, and comply with tax obligations.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage, cancel, or modify your subscription settings directly through Google Play's subscription management system.

For more information about how Google Play Billing handles your payment data, please review Google Play's privacy policy and terms of service.

6. Job Search and Third-Party Integrations

Resume AI Pro integrates with Jooble and Adzuna to provide access to live job listings across 66+ countries. When you:

  • Search for jobs: Your search filters (location, keywords, salary, job type) are used to retrieve relevant listings from these platforms.
  • View job details: Job posting information is displayed to you for review and analysis against your resume.
  • Apply to jobs via "Quick Apply": Your application is submitted directly to the job listing on the respective job board or employer site. We do not intercept or store these applications beyond tracking that you applied.

When you apply to a job, you are subject to the privacy policy of the job board or employer receiving your application. We recommend reviewing those policies independently.

7. Google Sign-In and Authentication

Resume AI Pro offers the option to sign in using your Google account for convenience. When you choose to use Google Sign-In:

  • Profile Information: We receive limited profile information from Google (name, email address, and profile picture) to create and manage your account.
  • Authentication: Google handles the secure authentication process. We do not receive or store your Google password.
  • Account Linking: You can disconnect your Google account from Resume AI Pro at any time through your account settings or Google's app permissions page.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share your Resume AI Pro data (resumes, job searches, scores) with Google or use Google Sign-In data for marketing beyond account management.

For information about how Google handles your sign-in data, please review Google's privacy policy.

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app:

  • Cloud storage and hosting providers (for secure data storage)

All service providers are contractually required to protect your data with the same level of security we apply.

8.2 With Your Explicit Consent

We will only share your resume, profile, or other personal information with third parties (such as employers or recruiters) if you explicitly authorize it.

8.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, or legal requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Protect the security or integrity of our services
  • Protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of Resume AI Pro, our users, or the public

8.4 Business Transfers

If Resume AI Pro is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

9. Data Security

We implement comprehensive technical, physical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal and resume data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms (password hashing, Google Sign-In integration)
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy best practices
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems

However, no security system is completely impenetrable. While we strive to protect your information with industry-standard security practices, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Account and Resume Data: Retained while your account is active. You can delete your account and associated data at any time.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription information is retained as long as you maintain an active subscription, plus a period for billing and tax compliance purposes.
  • Application Tracking Data: Retained for as long as you keep it in your dashboard; you can manually delete individual entries.
  • Usage and Log Data: Typically retained for 12 months for security and analytics purposes.
  • Backup Data: We maintain secure backups for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes.

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or permanently anonymize it. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
